About Us

When I say 'About Us' I actually mean 'About Me' but having a team sounds cool so lets go with that. GGWP is a site in which gamers can come to read reviews with ease. For now, there is only a limited amount of games (League of Legends, Overwatch, and PUBG) in the database, but hopefully it will grow as I work on it more. My goal is to have reviews on different patches, updates, and upcoming games written in a very humorous and relatable manner. For now, I have to settle with four games and pre-written reviews from IGN or Polygon until it actually becomes an 'Us'.

Otherwise, I am a student in Parsons School of Design who wants to become a front end coder and a UI designer. Sites like these are my first steps towards my goal.
